nehne Perfumer's Notebook is a web app — no app store needed.
Follow the steps below for your phone.

iPhone Android

iPhone

Uses Safari

Installing doesn't work from Chrome or in-app browsers (such as LINE or Instagram). Open this page — or the app itself — in Safari.

1

Open the app in Safari

Tap the "Open the app" button at the bottom of this page.

2

Tap the Share button

Tap (the square with an arrow) at the bottom center of the screen.

3

Choose "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down the menu, tap Add to Home Screen, then tap Add in the top right. That's it.

Android

Uses Chrome

A Google Play release is on the way. Until then, you can start using the app right now with the steps below.

1

Open the app in Chrome

Tap the "Open the app" button at the bottom of this page.

2

Tap the menu (⋮)

Open the three-dot menu in the top right corner.

3

Choose "Install app"

Tap Install app (on some phones, Add to Home screen), then Install. That's it.

Once it's added

Launch the app anytime from its icon on your home screen. It works offline, and your formulas are stored on your own device.

Switching to a new phone? Use the backup feature in "My Formulas" inside the app.
